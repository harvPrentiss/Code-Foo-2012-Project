A Little About Mario

Here is a little information about the Mario world in case you've never heard about it. Super Mario is the video game series that is best known for launching Nintendo. The games star Mario, a plumber, who has to save Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser. Bowser is always kidnapping the princess. There have been many games in the series and they're still being made today. Mario is one of the most recognizable characters in video games and serves as Nintendo's mascot.
